### Completion looks like:

```
> cli-languages/
  > indo-european/
    > latin.txt
    > germanic/
      > icelandic.txt
      > norwegian.txt
      > anglo-frisian/
        > english.txt
      > low-franconian/
        > dutch.txt
    > romance/ 
```

Once completed, enter the text in dutch.txt into Google Translate.
